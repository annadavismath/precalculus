$f(x)=\frac{x-1}{x^2+10x-24}$

$y$-intercept: $(0, \answer{\frac{1}{24}})$

$x$-intercept: $(\answer{1},0)$

$f(x)=\frac{x}{x^2+9}$

$y$-intercept: $(0, \answer{0})$

$f(x)=\frac{x^2-1}{x-2}$

Vertical Asymptote: $x=\answer{2}$

$y$-intercept: $(0, \answer{\frac{1}{2}})$
